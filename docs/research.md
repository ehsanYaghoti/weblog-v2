# Research

## Reference Websites

### DEV Community

https://dev.to

Things I like

- Simple and easy UI

- (Discover , Following , Latest) filter on home page
- making landing page like some articles page
- interesting article cards layout
- simple time filter on home page
- Article series
- multi and emoji like reactions
- Clean article layout
- Reading time
- Tag system
- Simple editor

Things I don't like

- Navbar on left side of home page
- Homepage feels crowded

### Medium

https://medium.com

Things I like

- Home page : for you tab (reason that why we chosed this article for you).
- Home page : article card I am not interested in this article option button (or mute publication , author , topics ).
- Excellent typography
- Custom and shareable libraries (save collections)
- Reading experience
- Minimal UI

Things I don't like

- Paywall
- Weak category discovery
